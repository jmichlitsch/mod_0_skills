class staff

name_of_worker (string)
is_the_worker_tipped (boolean)
salary_per_hour (int)
managers_and_underlings (hash/object)
number_working (int)

change_worker (changes name of name_of_worker)
add_pay_rate (adds 1 to salary_per_hour)
promote_manager (add another group and move an underling to manager in managers_and_underlings)
remove_number_working (remove a staff member from current number_working)
